WAR AND PEACE

Leo Tolstoy

Historical Fiction

★★★★☆ 4.0
Leo Tolstoy's War and Peace is a monumental novel depicting Russian society during Napoleon's invasion of Russia in the early 19th century, intertwining the lives and relationships of several noble families with epic historical events. Through characters like Pierre Bezukhov and Natasha Rostov, the novel explores profound themes of love, war, family, and the complexities of the human condition against the backdrop of historical upheaval.
